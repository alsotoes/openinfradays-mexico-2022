About OID Mexico

OpenInfra Days is an event organized by Open Infrastructure User Group around the world. And focused on Open Infrastructure Projects and any related topics.

OpenInfra Days Mexico is one of the regional OpenInfra Days. the event is organized by Open Infrastructure User Groups from several regions in Mexico. And it's focused on community development across Mexico. To union community efforts, to develop Open Infrastructure community, and to provide back to each local user groups in Mexico.
